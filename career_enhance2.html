Empowering Newcomers' Careers

Career Change / Enhance

Designed to prepare newcomers to take their careers on a different direction or to a higher level. This highly customized plan consists of nine steps, they are:

  • Resume Makeover
  • Cover Letter
  • Unique Value Proposition
  • Skills Inventory
  • Target Markets
  • Networking and Info Interview Practices
  • LinkedIn Makeover and Strategy
  • Cold Calling
  • Interview Preparation

In any case, your starting point working with CoachRod will be with the Intake Meeting & Resume Critique. Your investment on this initial step will be later discounted if you wish to join one of the Career Strategies designed by CoachRod. You can also select individual steps from the Career Strategies at any time, if that's your case.
