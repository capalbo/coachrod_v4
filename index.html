Empowering Newcomers' Careers

Introducing CoachRod


CoachRod brings a new perspective to career and life coaching. Focused on ensuring newcomers succeed and achieve their full potential in Canada, CoachRod is led by its founder and President - Rodrigo Porto, an experienced HR-T&D professional and career coach with experiences over 3 continents.

Currently a Manager for MBA Careers at the prestigious Sauder School of Business, UBC, in Vancouver, Canada; he brings his international diverse professional experience to assist, prepare, and develop MBA candidates from a top 100 B-school reach their career goals.

CoachRod offers a vast array of services based on 3 customized career strategies. These strategies focus on ensuring newcomers to Canada succeed in their careers and are designed to assist you in different phases of your relocation to Canada: Pre-Arrival, Landed, and Career Enhance/Change. They all have a common starting point, the Intake Interview and Resume Critique.

Your relationship with CoachRod starts here. Based on your intake interview and a review of your resume, CoachRod will recommend what is the best strategy and discuss what is next. You can also choose, after the Intake Meeting & Resume Critique, to work and develop a specific skill rather than choosing an entire career plan.

To learn more about your Starting Point with CoachRod, The Intake Meeting & Resume Critique ask CoachRod. Whatever choice or your career moment is, he will ensure you are ready and confident to take on your next professional challenge.

Your investment with this initial step will be discounted if you choose to proceed to one of the Career Strategies below.
