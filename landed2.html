Empowering Newcomers' Careers

Landed

Conceptualized for newcomers who have already landed in Canada and are in their first year. The Landed Plan will empower you to take control of your job search, have a strong digital presence, and feel confident to seek information interviews, attend networking events, learn what government services would be more appropriate for you and to be job interview ready.

The steps are:

  • Resume Makeover
  • Cover Letter Prep
  • UVP - Unique Value Proposition
  • Info Interview Techniques
  • Cold Calling
  • Government Services
  • Networking
  • Your target markets
  • LinkedIn makeover
  • Interview Preparation
